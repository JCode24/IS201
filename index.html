Our Story

The Bay View Tennis Academy was founded in 2023 by three young individuals who had just finished their college tennis careers. Based in Southern California, the academy looks to identify top talent within the area at a young age and provide those individuals with the training needed to not only play college tennis, but even play professionaly. A revival of American tennis is just around the corner, and these young men believe they can fill the holes that they identified in their junior careers to help the next generation of American talent make it to the top.
